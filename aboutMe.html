More About Me

   I am currently still in school, but I am already trying to implement myself into the tech industry through IBM. I am currently enrolled into the IBM Mainframe Pre-Apprenticeship which is my second IBM lessons I have taken. I'm not zoning down into only mainframes, but they are a small field that has a need for people. I currently have nine badges on Credly from IBM and Coursera, and by the end of the year I plan to have a total of twelve badges after successfully finishing the Pre-Apprenticeship.

   In addition to IBM, I also work part-time at Walmart in the pickup department for extra money along with a side business on etsy with my friend. At Walmart, I walk around the store grocery shopping for customers who ordered their items online, and then I deliver it to their car when they arrive or to an external delivery driver. On etsy, we sell custom-made banners which I design. I am the designer who makes the banners on Adobe Illustrator, and then he handles the logistics of the company. We currently only sell military welcome home banners, but are soon expanding to birthday and other celebration banners.
